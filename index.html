About Very Berry Kitchen

Very Berry Kitchen is a small boutique pastry studio in Phuket that creates desserts for people who love sweets and care about health at the same time.

We use activated green buckwheat, alternative sweeteners and carefully selected ingredients so that every slice feels light, balanced and full of flavor.
